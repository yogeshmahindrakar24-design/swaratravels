Places to Visit in Solapur

Akkalkot

Swami Samarth Temple — Akkalkot

📍 Akkalkot, Solapur District · 40 km from Solapur

This temple is dedicated to Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj, believed to be an incarnation of Lord Dattatreya. Devotees believe that visiting Akkalkot fulfills wishes and removes life problems. Swami Samarth lived here for many years and performed miracles.

✨ Why Famous: Powerful spiritual energy, miracles of Swami Samarth, huge devotee following.

Pandharpur

Vitthal Rukmini Temple — Pandharpur

📍 Pandharpur · 75 km from Solapur

Pandharpur is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Maharashtra dedicated to Lord Vitthal (Krishna). Lakhs of devotees visit during Ashadhi Ekadashi Yatra.

✨ Why Famous: Lord Vitthal temple, Wari pilgrimage, spiritual capital of Maharashtra.

Siddharameshwar

Siddharameshwar Temple

📍 Solapur City · Solapur City

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is located in the middle of Siddheshwar Lake. It was built by Saint Siddharama and is a peaceful spiritual spot.

✨ Why Famous: Lake temple, Mahashivratri fair, calm spiritual environment. 📲 Book Cab for This Temple
Tuljapur

Shree Tulja Bhavani Temple Tuljapur

📍 Tuljapur · 45 km from Solapur

The Shree Tulja Bhavani Temple is not just a place of religion. This Tulja Bhavani temple Shows serves as a cultural centre for the community. This is where thousands of pilgrims come for festivals like Diwali and Navratri. The Faith on this holy site connects people together during these celebrations. Tulja Bhavani's heavenly force is celebrated by everybody. Yoga and meditation are also practiced in the temple. Spiritual seekers from all over the world are drawn to it. Devotees develop a strong feeling of community as a result. This is where people gather for devotion and worship. The temple is a living history as well as a place of worship. To honour Goddess Tulja Bhavani, devotees congregate. They recall her protection and kindness. The temple's magnificent architecture and rich cultural heritage inspire courage and optimism. Every prayer offered is a call to the goddess. She guides her devotees through life’s challenges. Her compassion and power light their path. Her blessings nurture our spirit. She leads us toward justice, strength, and peace. Each and every prayer is an appeal to the goddess. She helps her followers overcome obstacles in life. Their road is made easier by her strength and kindness. Our spirits are nourished by her blessings. She guides us towards peace, justice, and power.
